Guild History

The Shadow Stalkers (SSBH) possess a rich and extensive bounty hunter history from the SWG universe beginning on Corbantis then stemming to the Bria and Starsider servers, dating back to initial game launch in 2003 and ending with the close of the Sony SWG servers in 2011. SSBH was the only exclusive BH guild that existed and consistently hunted throughout all three eras of SWG (precu, cu, nge), and became the only remaining exclusive bounty hunting guild by the end of NGE. Due to this fact, SSBH recorded thousands of confirmed kills and became the most recognized and infamous name in SWG bounty hunting. When it comes to SWG, we at SSBH eat, breath, and sleep player bounty hunting.


SSBH Announcement (September 1st, 2017)


Greeting Hunters! After spending much time on the SWGEmu Basilisk (Precu) server awaiting Jedi launch, the SSBH leadership is pleased to announce that we have decided to shift our focus to the SWG Legends Server (NGE) and ultimately launch SSBH once more. The SWG Legends server has been a successful and thriving server for nearing a year and a half and is generally a fully functioning NGE server as we remember it; that includes bounty hunting! The SSBH leadership has recently began creating and preparing toons on the SWG Legends Server and plans to re-launch SSBH once our toons are ready to hunt. Now is a great time to join us and get leveled/geared out. You can find us by downloading Discord (our voice chat/forum) and logging in under https://discord.gg/ez2SKba. This will remain an open voice chat and forum for the time being for anyone interested in joining SSBH, bounty hunting, or SWG Legends. -Eternal- (SWG Legends), SSBH Guild Master
